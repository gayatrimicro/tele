At Psych Care Live you can have online consultation sessions and you will be able to communicate, via video call, with one of our expert mental health providers using a smartphone, tablet or a computer. Find out how easy it is to receive affordable, quality care wherever you may be.


Why Psych Care Live?


Almost half of the adults in the United States will experience a mental illness during their lifetime. Less than half of the people who had a mental disorder in the past year received professional health care or other mental health services. Psych Care Live has given patients a way to see an online mental health provider in the comfort of their own home or office.

Increasing access to health care with Tele-psychiatry.


Are you one of the many people unsure of taking the next step in seeking mental health care because your anxiety, depression, or other symptoms make interacting with the world around you difficult? We at Psych Care Live give you peace of mind knowing you don’t have to travel long distances, lose time at work, or exit your comfort zone to get the help you need.

Anxiety, Depression, Emotional distress?

OR any other mental health problems? Here is what to do...

Call Us at (888) 688-4161

You will get connected to our Care Coordinator who will confirm information and review your condition as well as collect past medical history.

Our Care coordinator will connect you to the mental health provider.

The mental health provider will speak to you using secure computer technology and it is as similar as an in-person appointment.

Get easily accessible and immediate care.

Mental health provider will put your notes in electronic health records. Our care coordinator then will review diagnosed instructions, schedule follow-up visits if necessary and send a secure email with visit summary and follow-ups.
